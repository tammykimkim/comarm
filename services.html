Best Practices

Solutions Support Supervision

Compliance Supervision Services:

Comarm advises Canadian investment firms on Compliance best practices in all aspects of their business.

Compliance Support Services:

Comarm provides investment dealers, mutual fund dealers, and other securities market participants with support to meet all of their regulatory obligations, including:

  • Development of policies and procedures for new lines of business
  • Annual updating of policies and procedures
  • Annual training of employees for AML, Privacy, Chinese Walls (aka “Information Barriers”), and Comset (IIROC Rule 3100)
  • “Supervisor” training at all levels
  • Reviewing supervision processes to improve dealer results
  • Preparing for regulatory audits
  • Correcting regulatory audit deficiencies and responding to regulatory audit examination letters
  • Registration services

New IIROC/MFDA Member Registration Applications 

Comarm provides full support for new investment firms who want to register with IIROC or the MFDA. We will work with the firm’s principals to advise them and prepare the application to meet regulatory requirements.

Registration Support Services

Comarm provides investment firms with ongoing registrations support to ensure registration filings are current, continuing education requirements are met, and other services.

Anti-Money Laundering Solutions

Comarm assists investment firms with the following AML services:

  • Development of an AML risk assessment process per FINTRAC and FINCEN Guidelines
  • Development of risk mitigation strategies based on a balanced risk assessment process
  • Written AML policies and procedures
  • AML training for all applicable employees
