Comarm Solutions is a compliance consulting firm based in Toronto, Canada

that provides advice and assistance to securities dealers, mutual fund dealers, exempt market dealers, and other securities market participants.

About Us

As consultants, our depth of knowledge is unparalleled, and our backgrounds include many years of experience in compliance and risk management with securities dealers and regulators. The members of our team have worked with many different types of investment firms – large, national dealers to small, regional, wealth management businesses.

We recognize that each business is unique and management has defined its own objectives.

  • Our first goal is to listen to our clients
  • Our second goal is to bring our skills and experience to assist our clients in the most effective way for their business
